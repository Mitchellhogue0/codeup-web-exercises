National Parks FAQ

How many National Parks are there?
There are 423 National Park Sites
What can you do at National Parks?
Depending on the park, you could camp, fish, and other fun outdoor activities!
What is the most visited national park?
Great Smoky Mountains National Park
How old is the National Park System?
The act was signed by President Woodrow Wilson on August 25, 1916
Is there an overarching system over all parks?
Yes, the National Park Service
What is the origin of the National Park Service arrowhead?
The arrowhead was authorized as the official National Park Service emblem by the Secretary of the Interior on July 20, 1951
What is the largest national park site?
Wrangell-St. Elias National Park and Preserve, AK, at 13.2 million acres
What is the smallest national park site?
Thaddeus Kosciuszko National Memorial, PA, at 0.02 acres
How many people visit the national parks?
Total recreation visitors to the national parks in 2019: 327,516,619.
How do I make reservations for camping/lodging in national parks?
For campground reservations, visit Recreation.gov.
